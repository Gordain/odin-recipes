Butternut Soup

Description

A creamy curry butternut soup with a inlay of sour cream.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of butter.
  • 2 onions chopped.
  • 4 cloves of garlic chopped.
  • 300mls of chicken stock.
  • 900g of butternut squash cubed.
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder.
  • 1 teaspoon of salt.
  • Half teaspoon of ground cumin.
  • Quarter teaspoon of cayenne pepper.
  • 170g of single cream.
  • 2 tablespoons of honey.
  • 100g of sour cream.

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large saucepan and brown onions and garlic.
  2. Stir in chicken stock ans squash. Season with curry powder, salt, cumin and cayenne. Simmer until squash is tender.
  3. Stir in cream and honey, puree soup until smooth.
  4. Serve with an inlay of sour cream.
