Lasagna Recipe

Description

A fantastic Italian pasta dish, filled with lean ground beef mixed with herbs, onion and garlic inter layered with pasta sheets.

Ingredients

  • 450g of Italian sausage.
  • 340g lean ground beef.
  • 120g minced onion.
  • 2 cloves of garlic.
  • 800g of chopped tomatoes.
  • 185g of tomato sauce.
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato paste.
  • 120g of water.
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar.
  • 4 tablespoons of chopped parsley.
  • 2 teaspoons of basil.
  • 1.5 teaspoon of salt.
  • 1 teaspoon of italian seasoning.
  • half teaspoon of fennel seeds.
  • quarter teaspoon of black pepper.
  • 12 lasagna sheets.
  • 450g of ricotta cheese.
  • 300g of mozzarella cheese.
  • 300g of grated parmesan cheese.
  • 1 egg.

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic till well browned.
  2. Stir in chopped tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons of parsley, basil, tsp of salt, pepper, fennel seeds and italian seasoning. Simmer for one hour, stiring occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of water to boil, add salt and lasagna sheets for 10 mins, drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a bowl combine ricotta cheese, the egg, 2 tablespoons of parsley and half teaspoon of salt.
  5. Preheat oven to 190 degrees C (180 fan).
  6. To assemble, in a large baking dish, layer the tomato sauce, lasagna sheets, cheese mixture and sliced mozzarella. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 30 mins, rest lasagna before serving.
